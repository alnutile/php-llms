# How to Be a Good Teammate in Pickleball

1. **Communicate clearly on the court**
2. **Know your position and stick to it**
3. **Be supportive and encouraging**
4. **Call the ball to avoid collisions**
5. **Stay positive, even when losing**
6. **Be ready to receive serves and returns**
7. **Learn your partner's strengths and weaknesses**
8. **Cover the middle when your partner is pulled wide**
9. **Practice together to improve teamwork**
10. **Be a good sport, win or lose**
